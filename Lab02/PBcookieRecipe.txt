Peanut butter cookies
Attribution: http://allrecipes.com/recipe/10275/classic-peanut-butter-cookies/

1 C unsalted butter
1 C crunchy peanut butter
1 C white sugar
1 C packed brown sugar
2 eggs
2.5 C all-purpose flour
1 tsp baking powder
0.5 tsp salt
1.5 tsp baking soda

Directions
1. Cream butter, peanut butter, and sugars together in bowl; beat in eggs.

2. In separate bowl, sift flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt; 
stir into butter mixture. Put dough in refrigerator for 1 hr.

3. Roll dough into 1" balls and put on baking sheets. Flatten each ball with a fork,
making a crisscross pattern. 

4. Bake in preheated 375 deg oven for ~ 10 min or until cookies begin to brown.

Note: try topping with chocolate drizzle?
